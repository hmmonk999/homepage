About Me

When I'm not studying or participating in school activities, I'm probably writing. Writing has been a passion of mine since the 6th grade when my teacher assigned a creative writing project. Since then I've gotten better at creative writing, as well as technical writing, and in the future I would like to publish my own digital, interactive children's books.

From:

A small town just outside Champaign, Illinois

(Go Illini!)


Fun Fact:

I played travel softball for 10 years. For 8 of those years, I traveled an hour away from home to get to practices. I was a catcher. One day I would like to coach.

I am constantly trying to teach myself things. Two of my favorites subjects to research are NASA's discoveries about planets and the history of pirates. While those are my favorites to learn about, I try to teach myself things more relevant to my major too, like: user-experience research, programming langauages, WCAG Accessibility Standards, and more. Currently, I am teaching myself JavaScript and I am slowly building up my Japanese vocabulary (which is currently about a toddler's level of words). In the future, I would like to visit Tokyo. I love traveling. Learning about the history and culture of other places facinates me. I've been to 10 countries and 18/50 states and will continue to travel in the future.

I'm an Iowa State student majoring in technical communication and minoring in digital media and music technology. I think my major fits me well because it allows me to study rhetoric and write a lot, while also allowing me the freedom to study more creative course, like music and design. I am thinking about continueing my studies and going to grad school for human computer interaction.
