Instructions

To play each instrument, you must have a keyboard. The white text is the corrsponding key on the key board that activates the sound of the instrament. The yellow text is the note each part of the instrament plays.

To play the white keys on the piano, press the bottom row of lettered keys, z-m.
To play the black keys, press XXXX - s, f, g, j, or k.
To play the guitar strings, press the number keys 1-6.

*Sound effects come from Free Wave Samples, Internet Archive, and Sample Focus.
